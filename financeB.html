Finance B

Checkout some information on the Finance B Cluster! More resources available soon :)


Kavya Kaushal
Cluster Leader

Finance B consists of 4 individual objective tests in which competitors are allowed 60 minutes to complete the 100 question assessment. Like its name suggests, Finance B covers various topics which help students understand the basics on managing money involving the calculations based around it, how to keep them secure, and trends seen in the world. Finance B includes the following competitions: Business Calculations, Economics, Introduction to Financial Math, and Securities and Investments.
