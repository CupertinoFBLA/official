American Enterprise

The purpose of the American Enterprise project is to help members learn more about the system under which we live in and to share our expertise in some way with others inside and/or outside of the school. We promote small businesses within our community and prepare the next generation of Future Leaders by educating the youth on valuable business skills.


Janvi Prasad
American Enterprise Project Chair

Hello! I’m Janvi, a sophomore at Cupertino High and am the American Enterprise Project officer this year. As the AE Projects Chair, I am in charge of a team that works with local businesses, supporting them with marketing initiatives that include social media promotions. The team also volunteers and educates the surrounding community. In my free time, I enjoy dancing, capturing moments through photography, and spending time with my family and friends. I look forward to new opportunities and spending time with the team. Feel free to reach out with any questions!
