What is FBLA?


Future Business Leaders of America, commonly known as FBLA, is the largest student business organization in the world with over 250,000 members. It is designed to help students develop a wide variety of skills that nurture personal and professional growth.

Cupertino FBLA is the largest business student organization at Cupertino High School. We welcome students of all different backgrounds, interests, and skill levels to join our community and share in our passion for all aspects of business, including accounting, technology, and presentation! Our meetings take place on Thursdays at lunch.
